Malaysia Investment Symposium (MIS)

President
June 2022 – October 2022 | Bursa Young Investor Club, TARUMT KL

I conceived MIS during my first week of leadership at BYIC, then recruited a 100+ undergraduate committee to design and deliver the symposium at TARUMT.

  • Built the committee within 2–3 weeks; assembled a dynamic team of 100+ student leaders.
  • Delivered full registration within eight weeks: 500 participants.
  • Secured RM200,000 in cash and in-kind sponsorships to fund event delivery.
  • Hosted industry speakers across markets and themes, including RHB Investment Bank, UOB Asset Management, Wealthfort, and FPAM (VIP guest + opening speaker).
  • Achieved national reach: participation from 23 universities across Malaysia.
  • Maintained execution discipline through three meetings per week and a guiding principle: “If the plan doesn’t work, change the plan, but never change the goal.”
  • Received recognition as a milestone corporate event for TARUMT and a benchmark for BYIC Malaysia.

The event turned a new idea into a repeatable standard for corporate symposium delivery at TARUMT and beyond.
