Overview

As a Limited Partner Specialist at S&P Global, I supported institutional investors across Asia-Pacific by managing complex private market investment data and reporting processes. My clients included retirement funds, asset management companies, insurance companies, trust banks and development banks located across Hong Kong, Singapore, Australia, Japan, Korea and the Philippines.

I was entrusted with managing the firm's largest institutional client portfolio, representing more than US$550 billion in Assets Under Management across more than 1,000 private market funds. My work focused on ensuring investment data accuracy, improving operational efficiency, and maintaining long-term client satisfaction through proactive stakeholder engagement.
