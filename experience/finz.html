Founder of FINZ: Financial Education & Market Insights

FINZ is a financial education and market insights platform that I founded in 2020 to bridge the knowledge gap in Malaysia’s capital markets. The name FINZ reflects my motivation: “FIN” for finance and “Z” for Generation Z—driving my goal to deliver financial literacy and market insights to younger investors.

I managed and operated FINZ from 2020 to 2023, then stepped back to focus on postgraduate studies in the United Kingdom. During that period, the platform served as a channel to provide up-to-date financial news and educational content tailored to retail investors.

FINZ’s education approach used a top-down structure—starting from macroeconomic trends, then moving into industry analysis, company fundamentals, and technical analysis—so retail investors could understand both the broader market environment and practical methods for analysing stocks.

Financial News Alert Service

Provided a free alert service sharing timely financial news relevant to market-moving events and updates that could impact broad markets and individual stocks.

Company Analysis Reports

Offered one-page company analysis reports designed for busy retail investors, covering comppany business model, future prospects, fundamental qualities, and technical analysis for short- and long-term perspective.

Stock Discovery & Recommendations

Helped investors identify potential high-growth stocks and improve risk–reward decisions to pursue more stable, better-informed returns.

Over four years, FINZ gained traction and grew to a community of 2,600+ followers. I also recruited two additional team members to expand capacity and improve delivery for subscribers.
