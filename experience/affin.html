Role & Impact

Working as a Proprietary Trader Intern, I collaborated with senior traders to optimize trading approaches and execute high-frequency trades. A core part of my work was conducting in-depth technical analysis on the top five traded stocks each day, focusing on trends and price patterns to produce actionable insights for the trading desk.

I also helped keep the desk informed by disseminating timely updates on global financial news, government policies, and company reports to more than 40 trading professionals, ensuring strategies reflected the latest market developments.

Alongside senior traders, I supported daily order management for over 100 buy/sell orders by focusing on execution quality, bid-offer spread considerations, and strategic market positioning. This internship strengthened my financial modeling, risk awareness, and decision-making under real-time pressure.
