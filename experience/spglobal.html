Overview

As a Limited Partner Specialist at S&P Global, I supported institutional investors across Asia-Pacific by managing complex private market investment data and reporting processes. My clients included retirement funds, asset management companies, insurance companies, trust banks, and development banks located across Hong Kong, Singapore, Australia, Japan, Korea, and the Philippines.

I was entrusted with managing the firm's largest institutional client portfolio, representing more than US$550 billion in Assets Under Management across more than 1,000 private market funds. My work focused on ensuring investment data accuracy, improving operational efficiency, and maintaining long-term client satisfaction through proactive stakeholder engagement.

Investment Data Lifecycle

Fund Onboarding

Managed onboarding of private market funds, including GP consent, while ensuring data completeness and reporting accuracy.

Fund Level Data Management

Managed fund-level data, including Schedule of Investment verification and end-to-end reporting readiness checks.

Cash Transactions

Maintained capital call and distribution data, reconciling total capital calls and distributions to GP reporting and inside/outside commitment alignment.

NAV Reconciliation

Validated NAV reporting by excluding post-cash transactions and adjusting for transactions that did not impact NAV.

Performance Monitoring

Tracked IRR alongside quarter-level performance monitoring, remaining capital commitment, and portfolio exposure.

Portfolio Company Data

Maintained operational and investment metrics for underlying portfolio companies through the DataBridge template.

Stakeholder Engagement

Worked directly with institutional clients to resolve data variance, reporting issues, and investment data discrepancies.

Client Onboarding & Offboarding

Supported end-to-end client onboarding and offboarding processes, ensuring smooth transitions and continuity of service.

Private Equity Index

Supported the launch of S&P Global's Private Equity Index by contributing to fund-level and portfolio company data migration and integration.
