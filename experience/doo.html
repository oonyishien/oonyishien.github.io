Overview

As an Operations Executive under the Management Trainee Program at Doo Group, I rotated across six critical departments: Client Management, Trading & Dealing, Financial Operations, Settlement, Support, and Risk Management. This cross-departmental exposure equipped me with a broad understanding of the company’s operating model before specialising further within the Operations Department.

The role provided hands-on exposure to client onboarding, trading system configuration, operational testing, fund movement processes, internal reconciliations, exchange rate updates, and multi-regional operational workflows. Through this experience, I developed a stronger understanding of how front-end client requirements, trading infrastructure, and back-office operations connect within a financial services environment.

Core Responsibilities

Client Management

Responsible for onboarding individual, corporate, and institutional clients globally, ensuring smooth account setup processes while adhering to regulatory and compliance standards across different regions.

Full Client Onboarding Cycle

Managed the full client onboarding cycle, from account opening to implementing rebate structures tailored to diverse client requirements, supporting stronger client satisfaction and operational efficiency.

Trading System Configuration

Participated in the configuration and maintenance of trading systems, including Product Center and Upper Broker platforms, helping ensure system integrity across multiple regions.

System Testing & Integration

Provided operational support for testing and integrating new features into trading systems, ensuring alignment with both client and broker requirements.

UAT & PRD Testing

Contributed to user acceptance testing and production system testing to ensure smooth functionality, identify potential issues, and support full-scale implementation readiness.

Fund-In & Fund-Out Operations

Oversaw fund-in and fund-out processes, ensuring accurate and timely internal transfers, reconciliations, and fund management for both retail and institutional clients.

Exchange Rate Management

Managed and updated exchange rates for clients, supporting accurate and compliant financial transactions within a multi-currency operating environment.

Operational Coordination

Worked across multiple operational functions to understand how client-facing service, trading infrastructure, and back-office financial processes interact within the wider business.

Cross-Department Learning

Key Takeaways

My time at Doo Group strengthened my understanding of financial operations from both a client-facing and operational infrastructure perspective. The rotation across six departments helped me understand how onboarding, trading systems, financial operations, settlement, support, and risk management work together to deliver reliable services to global clients.

This experience also enhanced my ability to work across teams, understand system requirements, support operational testing, and manage financial processes in a fast-moving multi-currency environment. It provided a strong foundation for my later work in institutional investment data management and private market operations.
