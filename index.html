About Me

I am a finance professional specialising in private equity, institutional investment data management, and investment analytics. My experience spans supporting global institutional investors across Asia-Pacific, including retirement funds, asset management firms, insurance companies, trust banks, and development banks.

At S&P Global, I managed investment data for one of the firm's largest institutional portfolios, representing more than US$550 billion in Assets Under Management (AUM) across over 1,000 private market funds. My responsibilities covered the complete investment data lifecycle, including fund onboarding, cash transaction processing, NAV reconciliation, capital commitment tracking, IRR monitoring, and portfolio company data management.

Beyond finance, I enjoy leveraging technology to solve business problems. This portfolio website was personally designed and developed from scratch using HTML and CSS as part of my continuous learning journey, reflecting my passion for combining finance with technology.
