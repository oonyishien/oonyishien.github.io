Guest Speaker & Panel Appearances

Sharing financial insights through guest speaking and panel participation—focused on financial literacy, investment strategy, and practical guidance for aspiring investors and traders.

Speaking Highlights

Three recent speaking moments where I contributed to discussions, engaged with audiences, and supported learning for the next generation of investors.

InvestSmart@SC (Securities Commission Malaysia) — Panelist

InvestSmart@SC • 10th Anniversary of BYIC • Hosted by BYIC TARUMT
Invited to join a distinguished panel to commemorate the 10th anniversary of the Bursa Young Investor Club (BYIC). The discussion focused on financial literacy and practical investment strategies for young investors.
  • Topics included early-stage investing, budgeting, and the importance of avoiding debt.
  • Panel moderated by Jay Er with panellists including Ting Jing Yuan, CFA, CQF and Sung Ming Chiam.
  • Event brought together industry professionals, students, and aspiring investors in collaboration with Bursa Malaysia, Securities Commission Malaysia, Ted Optimus, and Kenanga Investment Bank.

Malaysia Youth Trading Competition — Guest Speaker

Malaysia Stock Market • Trading education session
Invited to share trader-focused insights for aspiring young traders, covering market drivers, practical trading strategies, and discipline under market volatility in the Malaysian equity market.
  • Key drivers affecting listed stocks on Bursa Malaysia: economic indicators, sector trends, and market sentiment.
  • Practical strategies for opportunity identification and risk management in a volatile environment.
  • Trader psychology and the importance of maintaining discipline when navigating dynamic market conditions.
  • Highlighted how fundamentals support technical analysis to strengthen decision-making.

TARUC Investment Trading Competition — Guest Speaker

Investment decision-making • Novice-friendly guidance
Invited to share a structured approach to making informed investment decisions, helping participants connect business fundamentals with market trends and disciplined risk–reward thinking.
  • How to evaluate a company’s business model before investing.
  • Exploring current and future trends in the Malaysian stock market.
  • Sharing personal insights on selected companies using practical examples and case studies.
  • Guidance on balancing risk and reward with patience and discipline for long-term goals.
