Featured Panel & Speaking Sessions

Featured Panel: InvestSmart@SC (Securities Commission Malaysia)

I was invited as a guest panelist for InvestSmart@SC, celebrating the 10th anniversary of the Bursa Young Investor Club (BYIC). Hosted by BYIC TARUMT and organised in collaboration with Bursa Malaysia, Securities Commission Malaysia, Ted Optimus, and Kenanga Investment Bank, the event brought together industry professionals, students, and aspiring investors.

My panel contribution focused on practical financial literacy and investment strategies, including early-stage investing, budgeting, and why avoiding debt matters for long-term financial stability. The discussion was moderated by Jay Er, alongside panelists Ting Jing Yuan (CFA, CQF) and Sung Ming Chiam.

  • Presented and discussed approachable guidance for young investors on building good investing habits early.
  • Supported audience understanding by connecting investment strategy with responsible financial planning.
  • Engaged actively with participants, reflecting strong enthusiasm and engagement during the session.

I’m grateful to the organising team and sponsors for the opportunity to contribute, and I look forward to continuing my efforts in promoting financial literacy.

Malaysia Youth Trading Competition — Guest Speaker

Trading education • Bursa Malaysia youth event

Invited to share Trader Insights in the Malaysia Stock Market with aspiring young traders and finance enthusiasts. I covered market drivers, practical trading strategies, and the importance of discipline when navigating volatility.

  • Explained key drivers affecting Bursa Malaysia stocks: economic indicators, sector trends, and market sentiment.
  • Shared practical trading strategy principles, with emphasis on risk management in a volatile environment.
  • Discussed trader psychology and discipline to avoid impulsive decisions.
  • Connected fundamentals with technical analysis to strengthen decision-making.

The audience energy and curiosity reinforced my commitment to helping the next generation invest with informed confidence.

TARUC Investment Trading Competition — Guest Speaker

Investment decision-making • Case-led guidance

Invited to share how to make informed and strategic investment decisions in a dynamic financial landscape. The session focused on business model evaluation, market trends, and building disciplined risk–reward thinking.

  • Highlighted the importance of analysing a company’s business model before investing.
  • Reviewed current and future Malaysian market trends and key factors that shape stock performance.
  • Shared personal insights on selected companies using practical examples and case studies.
  • Concluded with guidance on balancing risk and reward through patience and discipline.

I’m thankful to the organising committee and participants, and I look forward to continuing to support young investors.
